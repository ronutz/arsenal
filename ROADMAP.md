- **v1.16 — 2026-07-01 (ANVIL, PRIME — new tool: cvss-vector-decoder, tool #36):** Shipped **cvss-vector-decoder** (rank 11 of the value queue), Security & WAF family. Paste a CVSS v3.0 or v3.1 vector (`CVSS:3.1/AV:N/AC:L/PR:N/UI:N/S:U/C:H/I:H/A:H`) and get the **Base score computed** and mapped to None / Low / Medium / High / Critical, the **Temporal and Environmental** scores when those metrics are present, the **Impact and Exploitability** sub-scores, the version and scope, and every metric spelled out in plain words.
